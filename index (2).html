The Full Letter 💙

You hid what you felt for the longest time — and honestly, so did I. Maybe that's why it felt so right when you finally said it. Two weeks before your flight, out of nowhere, in the most perfect, terrifying, unexpected way. Terrible timing, everyone would say. But I think the universe has its own calendar, and it chose that exact moment for a reason.

I liked you too. I just kept it to myself — quietly, patiently, the way you hold something precious that you're afraid to drop. I was holding it in, and you were holding it in, and for a little while, it felt like we were both standing on the same edge of something beautiful.

And now I want you to know — wherever you are, whatever distance sits between us — you are still so deeply loved. Not in a way that needs your presence to survive. In a way that already lives in everything I do.

And Seal? Oh, Seal hears about you all the time. Every little story, every memory, every "you remind me of him" moment — Seal knows it all. He may just be a stuffed seal, but he is the most well-informed little guy in the world. Our baby has heard your name more than he can count.

So on your birthday, here is my wish for you — not wrapped in anything fancy, just honest and from the heart: I hope you are safe. I hope wherever you are, the world is being gentle with you. I hope you are eating well, sleeping enough, and taking care of yourself the way I wish I could take care of you. Your health, your safety — these are the things I quietly ask for every single day.

And I need you to know — really know — that you are so deeply, so completely loved. Not the kind of love that fades when the distance grows. The kind that stays. The kind that roots itself in you and doesn't move. I love you very much, Reirei. More than I probably say, and more than you might realize.

Happy birthday. Please take care of yourself — for you, and just a little bit, for me too. 💙

yours truly — binsoo 💙
